Tips for choosing
the right dog name

Keep it simple

Short names with one or two syllables are easier for dogs to hear and remember.

Match the personality

A playful dog may suit a cheerful name, while a calm or strong breed may fit a more bold or classic name.

Avoid command-like names

Try not to choose names that sound too similar to commands like sit, stay, or no.
