About
Pawfect Companion

Pawfect Companion is a frontend-based informational website designed to provide essential knowledge about dogs. It helps users understand dog breeds, proper dog care practices, and creative dog naming ideas in a simple and organized way.

The website is built for dog owners, future pet owners, students, and animal lovers who want easy access to useful and reliable information. With a user-friendly and responsive design, Pawfect Companion makes learning about dogs more enjoyable and accessible.
